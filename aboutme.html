About Me

  • This is the story of the boy with the videos. It all started in 2018 when I discovered the incredible world of drones. At just 9 years old, I saved my allowance for a whole year to buy my first drone, a DJI Tello. The moment it arrived, my life changed. With every flight, I discovered a new world, seeing places in ways I had never imagined.
  • The drone’s camera fascinated me, and it didn’t take long before I created a YouTube channel where I shared videos about drones and my daily life. Two years later, I invested in a DJI Phantom, a more advanced drone, and that’s when my passion for filmmaking was born. It was no longer just about flying—it was about creating images that told stories.
  • At 12, I edited my first video for a neighbor and realized that my passion could become a profession. I started studying intensely, taking audiovisual courses, filming for local businesses, and building my portfolio. My dedication caught the attention of a filmmaker who invited me to shoot and edit weddings, where I learned a lot about the professional market.
  • Today, with two years of experience as the lead editor at a wedding production company, I am responsible for every detail of the videos, from editing to color grading. I also work as a freelancer, focusing on editing and color grading, always striving to exceed expectations and take each project to the next level.
  • My journey started with a drone and a dream. Today, I turn stories into videos that move and connect.
